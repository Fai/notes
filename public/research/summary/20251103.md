### Theoretical Advances

#### Transformer Injectivity (October 2025)
- **Finding**: Transformers proven to be "injective"
  - Unique inputs map to unique hidden states
  - Makes transformers theoretically invertible
- **Algorithm**: GLADIA Lab developed SiPIT
  - Can reconstruct exact prompts from hidden states
- **Implications**:
  - Enhanced AI interpretability
  - Security and privacy considerations
  - Potential for reverse-engineering prompts

#### Multimodal AI Advances
- **Google DeepMind**: Announced significant improvements
  - Enhanced joint understanding of visual, audio, and environmental data
