## Study Tips

### Before the Exam
1. **Understand the exam guide** - Know domain weights
2. **Hands-on practice** - Don't just watch videos
3. **Take practice exams** - Aim for 80%+ before scheduling
4. **Review wrong answers** - Understand why you got them wrong

### Exam Day
1. **Time management** - ~2 minutes per question
2. **Flag uncertain questions** - Review at end
3. **Read carefully** - Look for keywords (MOST, LEAST, BEST)
4. **Eliminate wrong answers** - Narrow to 2 options
5. **Trust AWS best practices** - When in doubt, choose AWS-native

### After the Exam
- Results within minutes (pass/fail)
- Detailed score report within 5 business days
- Digital badge via Credly
